Understanding Mental Health

Recognizing and understanding different mental health conditions is the first step toward healing and support.

Anxiety Disorders

Characterized by persistent, excessive worry and fear about everyday situations. Physical symptoms may include rapid heartbeat and sweating.

Depression

A mood disorder causing persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest, and impaired daily functioning.

PTSD

A condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing traumatic events, with symptoms including flashbacks, nightmares, and severe anxiety.

Bipolar Disorder

A mental health condition causing unusual shifts in mood, energy, activity levels, and concentration.

OCD

A disorder characterized by unreasonable thoughts and fears (obsessions) leading to repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

Eating Disorders

Serious conditions related to persistent eating behaviors that negatively impact health, emotions, and ability to function.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Mendora.

Is Mendora a replacement for traditional therapy?

Mendora is designed to complement traditional therapy, not replace it. While our AI can provide evidence-based support and techniques, we recommend working with a licensed professional for serious mental health concerns. Our Professional plan integrates human therapy with our AI tools for comprehensive care.

How secure and private is my data?

Your privacy is our top priority. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never share or sell your personal information. You can delete your data at any time, and our systems comply with HIPAA and GDPR requirements. For more details, please review our privacy policy.

Can I use Mendora in a crisis situation?

While Mendora offers crisis resources, it's not designed as an emergency service. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact a crisis hotline or emergency services immediately. Mendora can help direct you to appropriate crisis resources but should not be relied upon in life-threatening situations.

How does the AI therapy assistant work?

Our AI therapy assistant uses advanced natural language processing to engage in meaningful conversations about your mental health. It's trained on evidence-based therapeutic approaches like CBT, DBT, and ACT. The AI adapts to your needs over time, but always within clinically validated frameworks. It can suggest exercises, provide psychoeducation, and help you track progress.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. After cancellation, you'll continue to have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers.
